## What is Nafsi?

Nafsi is a desktop app that helps a psychiatry or psychotherapy practice keep its clinical record in one place — clients, sessions and notes, symptoms, assessments and instruments, diagnoses, treatments, medications, exercises, and psychoeducation material.

- **Offline-first.** Everything runs on your own machine. There is no cloud sync and no account to sign into — your patients' records never leave your computer.
- **Encrypted at rest.** The clinical database is encrypted on disk from the very first launch, and file attachments are encrypted per-file.
- **Trilingual and RTL-first.** The whole interface, and the seeded clinical catalog, work in Arabic, English, and French, with proper right-to-left layout.
- **Made for clinical reasoning.** Assessment-driven decision support (symptom → screener → diagnosis) that suggests, never auto-diagnoses; reusable treatment protocols; printable client and per-session PDF records.
- **Patient messaging.** Compose and send WhatsApp reminders through an editable, review-before-send step.

## Download & install

### macOS

1. Download the latest **`.dmg`** from the [Releases](https://github.com/Salman-001/nafsi-releases/releases/latest) page. One universal installer runs on both **Apple Silicon** and **Intel** Macs.
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Nafsi** into your **Applications** folder.
3. Launch it. The build is **signed and notarized by Apple**, so it opens with a normal double-click — no Gatekeeper warning and no right-click-to-open workaround.

**Requirements:** macOS 13 (Ventura) or later.

### Windows

1. Download the latest **`-setup.exe`** from the [Releases](https://github.com/Salman-001/nafsi-releases/releases/latest) page.
2. Run it. Windows SmartScreen may show a **"Windows protected your PC"** notice — click **More info → Run anyway** to continue. The installer adds Nafsi to your Start Menu and Desktop.

**Requirements:** Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit).

## Updates

Nafsi checks for updates automatically and offers them from inside the app — when a new version is available you'll see a prompt on the dashboard, and it installs and relaunches in place. You don't need to download a new installer by hand for each release. You can also check manually any time from **Settings → Updates**.

---

## Privacy & security

Nafsi is built for clinical confidentiality:

- No cloud, no account, no telemetry — data stays on your device.
- The clinical database is encrypted at rest; attachments are individually encrypted.
- Backups are encrypted with a password you set, so an exported backup file is safe to store off-machine.

## Reporting bugs & requesting features

Found a problem or have an idea? Please [open an issue](https://github.com/Salman-001/nafsi-releases/issues/new). Helpful details for a bug:

- Your Nafsi version (**Settings → About**) and your OS version.
- What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead.
- Screenshots if the issue is visual.

Please **do not include real patient information** in an issue — redact names and identifying details before posting.
